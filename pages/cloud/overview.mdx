# Private managed RealTimeX

RealTimeX cloud is the easiest way to trial and scale RealTimeX for your business or personal use.

The RealTimeX, Inc team offers **isolated** and **private** instances of RealTimeX that you can use to try out or scale up RealTimeX for your business or personal use.

Each instance is hosted on an isolated AWS instance that is automatically updated and managed by the RealTimeX, Inc core team. Your data and resources are not shared with any other customers who are using our managed service.
